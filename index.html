About Me

Hi, there! I'm Iyo Hirayama - hobbyist based in Ishikawa, Japan at this moment.
I love to draw pictures since I was a kid, and I prefer to draw cute things with vivid colors. Drawing is always a way to express my emotions for me. I had studied abroad in the US for a few years to study art and graphic design, so I miss pecan pies sometimes, haha. Also, I like to study anything I am interested in because I am full of curiosity. If you would have any questions or offers, please feel free to send me a contact.
